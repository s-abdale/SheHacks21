Play Food and Wine

Ottawa, ON

Star Rating

Phenomenal restaurant at the nation’s capital. Great food with excellent wine pairings. Stellar service where the staff effortlessly make you feel attended without hovering. I highly recommend this place for both the food and atmosphere.

Sushi California

Toronto, ON

Star Rating

Located at the West of Toronto, Sushi California is the best sushi restaurant in the city that I’ve been to so far. The food was mouth wateringly delicious and came in large portions! The staff was polite, and often checked on us. I’d give them a 5 rating! Looking forward to my next visit already.

Top Gun Burgers

Kensington Market (Toronto), ON

Star Rating

Sometimes you need to just indulge and satisfy your craving for a greasy burger and loaded fries and Top Gun Burgers definitely does that!Located in the heart of Kensington market, Top Gun Burgers is a tiny but cosy establishment. The staff are friendly and attentive but leave you alone enough for you to stuff your face in peace!
